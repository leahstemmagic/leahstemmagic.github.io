Temperature sensor research project:Data

Temperature VS. Emotion:
This graph shows the correlation of the temperature and the average emotions. As you can see our hypothesis was clearly wrong the temperature had little to no effect on students moods.

Period VS. Emotion:
This graphs shows the correlation of the period and the median emotion. The periods are in descending order from the beginning of the day until the end. Between 3rd and 4th period is lunch, which may contribute to the unusually happy emotions.

Day VS. Emotion:
This graph shows the correlation between the day and the temperature each number on the x axis represents a day. For example 1 is monday 2 is tuesday etc. There is a slight difference in emotion on different day.

Room VS. Emotion:
This graph shows the correlation between the room and the average emotion. Different rooms have slightly different median emotion.
