Temperature sensor research project

For this project we wanted to see if temperature affected students moood. We hypothesized that extremely hot or low temperatures would lead to more unhappy moods. To do this we constistently took the temperature of different classroom and asked students to rate their moods from 1-10; 10 being the happiest 1 being extremely unhappy and 5 being nuetral.
