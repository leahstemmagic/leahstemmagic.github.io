Temperature sensor research project: Is my school too cold?


For this project we wanted to see if temperature affected students mood. We hypothesized that extremely hot or low temperatures would lead to more unhappy moods. To do this we constistently took the temperature of different classrooms and asked students to rate their moods from 1-10; 10 being the happiest, 1 being extremely unhappy, and 5 being nuetral. From there we coverted the data into graphs.
